OUR VISION


We are established to provide Quality Education to under privileged Children and Women. In the next 1-2 years, the society projects and plans to reach and provide education to children in the Slums of Delhi and NCR. In the future the Trust see itself providing the Education in Rural and Urban areas through a range of interventions. To achieve this objective the Trust has plan of action to start with small Education Centre covering the maximum underprivileged Children and women in slum areas.
